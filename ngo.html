Welcome To The KEMO Organisation Website

KEMO Organisation was founded in 2028,by Peter Morara and Kelvin Morara.The aim of the organisation is to break the vicious cycle of poverty and social isolation to restore hope for a better future by raising funds internationally to help the needy thus bringing development. We believe that every person has the right to access resources and opportunities in order to live and develop with dignity and to become an active and contributing member of our society.
